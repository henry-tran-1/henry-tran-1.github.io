Bootcamp Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My goal is to become a software developer and the destination I have in mind is the health sector. I'm hoping to gain skills in both programming but also human skills that will make me a more attractive candidate for hiring.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I feel I had a good gauge on what my strengths and limitations were in the Foundations Learning plan.
I think my strengths in working well in a team environment will remain useful at bootcamp where I'll be with my peers in person. I do worry how I will handle the bootcamp when the course becomes more intense and the content becomes heavier.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Keeping up positive habits, having a healthy mindset and staying on top of the material. Bootcamp will run for a longer time, and will likely involve more difficult content and concepts. I worry that when I find the course to be too challenging, I may lose the momentum I've gained so far in Foundations.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to work on being more assertive and be better at giving and taking feedback.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

Simliar to in foundations, I have an expectation from the course and facilitators to help guide me in the right direction for my learning and to let me know if I'm no longer on this path. I would like to rely on them to help guide me through roadblocks I may encounter.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

My expectations from myself is to keep my mindset positive and open, and to continue working hard even when it's challenging.
For programming-related issues, I will first rely on the tech facilitators (during tech-ins or during the weekday when there is available time). For other issues, I have my family to rely on - they are a strong support system for me.
I will continue to devote my full attention to the DA course, and am committed to staying on top of all the material and recommended tech + human exercises.
I am commited to maintaining a positive attitude and openness with my fellow students and teachers, and all other people I encounter on this journey.

Foundations Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My goal is to become a software developer and work full time in the tech industry. I would like to eventually work again in the healthcare sector but as a programmer.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths: Good grit and determination; good initiative in seeking out information I want to know; work well in team environment.
Limitations: Tends to avoid confrontation; sometimes prone to being overwhelmed and losing confidence.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to develop better discipline and healthier study habits.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I am committed to adhering to the recommended timeboxes and content in the DA foundation and bootcamp. I will aim to stay up-to-date on learning competencies and not fall behind; and not forge ahead and risk burnout. I will be dedicated to attending the weekly homegroup meetings and find time to ask questions when I am struggling. If the things I am struggling with are technical-based, I will ask for help in my cohort discord, the foundation help desk, or facilitators. If the things I'm struggling with are more personal, I have a strong support system in my family whom I can rely on when needed.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

If I feel I'm unable to solve these issues with the above methods, I will alert the facilitators in a timely manner and look for other solutions together.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I have an expectation that the course and facilitators will help steer me in the right direction in my software development journey, and to let me know if I'm wandering away form this path. I would also like to use the facilitators as a way to help me move past mental blocks in my learning when I encounter them. Perhaps something that would have taken me a long time to learn on my own, may be expedited by someone who has been through similar issues.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

No major scheduling problems that I can forsee. I'm dedicating my full focus on the course.
